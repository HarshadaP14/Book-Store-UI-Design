Chetan Bhagat

Chetan Bhagat is one of India's most popular contemporary authors, known for his engaging and relatable storytelling. His novels often capture the pulse of modern Indian society.

Ruskin Bond is a beloved author known for his vivid storytelling and charming depictions of life in the Indian hill stations. His works are celebrated for their warmth and simplicity.

Arundhati Roy is an acclaimed author and activist, best known for her powerful and lyrical prose. Her debut novel received international acclaim and established her as a significant literary voice.
